Dub Hop

Project, UX, UI Design

September 2015 - December 2015

Description

DubHop is a mobile application design concept with the goal to help students easily discover events around campus based on location, time, and interested tags. Through a ten-week course HCDE 318: User-Centered Design, my team researched current issues with campus-discovery applications, created personas, scenarios, and storyboards to develop the idea of designing a mobile application. Then, we began to create sitemaps, wireframes, and prototypes to test features of our design, and created high-fidelity screens of the application.

Team Members: Karin Vaughan, Tongfang Sun

Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer

Tools: Wireframes, Moqups, OmniGraffle, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
