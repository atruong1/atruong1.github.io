UX Designer Intern

Description

At eNotes, I worked with a small team of eight people, including myself, in the Development team. Some of my projects included: creating banners to promote the subscription model, create breadcrumbs of links users go through, and redesigning the math textbook solutions page to make it more user-friendly.

Role: UX Designer, Developer

Tools: HTML, Sass, PHP, JavaScript, GitHub, Mac OSX Terminal
